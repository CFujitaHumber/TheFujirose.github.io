Fujiroses Tic-Tac-Toe

Purpose of this game is to create a simple, Tic-Tac-Toe game. It's currently in development with a team.

General Rules:

  • • Players take their turn placing a mark (either X or O) into one of the 9 slots.
  • • The first player will place “X” marks and the second will place “O” Marks.
  • • The first player to get 3 in a row will win the game.
  • • Players can only place one mark at a time
  • • Players cannot place a mark in a slot where there is one already.
  • • Players cannot move or trade other players marks.
  • • If there is no possibility to win on either side it is then a tie and the game is over.
